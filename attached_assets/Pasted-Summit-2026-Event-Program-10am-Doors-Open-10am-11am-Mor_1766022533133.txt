Summit 2026 - Event Program

10am:  Doors Open
10am - 11am:  Morning Check-in and Registration. Coffee/Juice/Snack Station, Networking.
11am - 3:30pm:  Daytime Program.  Two separate tracks.
Start-up School (Track 1).  Experts in fundraising, business formation, intellectual property rights, product development, branding, finance and more will be on site for small group breakouts and interactive workshops.  Geared towards aspiring founders who have a business idea, but want help to flesh it out!  Participants will be divided into four groups that will rotate around separate stations with subject matter experts across four periods.
11:00am:  Opening Remarks
Round 1 (11:10am - 11:55am)
Round 2 (12:00pm - 12:45pm)
12:50pm - 1:35pm - Lunch Break
Round 3 (1:40pm - 2:25pm)
Round 4 (2:30pm - 3:15pm)
Speaker Talks (Track 2). Listen to inspiring talks from some of Sarasota’s top founders and thinkers. Topics will include start-up exits, future trends, real-world applications of AI and how industries are evolving. Main stage, stadium style.
12 speaker talks for 20 minutes each, roughly 3 talks per hour. No Q&A.
1pm - 1:40pm - Lunch Break, roughly after 6 talks.
3:30pm - 5pm:  Pitching and Fundraising. Main stage
Investor Quick Pitch:  3:30pm - 4:20pm. Pitch your business idea to our panel of seasoned investors and operators, and get immediate feedback.
Summit participants can sign up for a 3 minute quick pitch on the main stage. Followed by 2 minutes of feedback from the panel of investors and operators.
VC Roundtable:  4:20pm - 5pm
Hear from a panel of seasoned VC’s about trends in the venture space and tactics for fundraising. How to weigh raising outside funds versus bootstrapping a business.
5pm - 6pm:  Evening Check-in, Food/drink, Networking
6pm - 8:30pm:  Evening program. Main stage and full ballroom, stadium style. Speakers and panels covering blue tech, the impact of AI and building in Sarasota.
6pm - 6:10pm:  Welcome and opening remarks
6:10pm - 6:40pm:  Keynote Speaker - CEO of Mote.  
Blue Tech - What's Next for Florida's Ocean Economy?  
6:45pm - 7:35pm:  Panel - Senior leaders at local enterprises. 
Challenges for the Enterprise - Innovating at Scale. Adapt or Die - Will your business survive the AI revolution?
7:40pm - 8:30pm:  Panel - Local start-up founders.  
Building in Sarasota - We can do it here! Challenges and opportunities - straight from our own start-up founders.
8:30pm - 10pm:  Afterparty.
Networking, alcohol drinks, light food
